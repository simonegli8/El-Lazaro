Granada, Granada, Nicaragua

  • katoutofhell

    Wow. Exactly one year ago today that I left the UK for the second part of my #solo backpacking adventure. First stop was Granada, Nicragua where I met this hilarious and amazing soul; John Oliver (wrote about him previously; a former guerilla soldier who lived by the motto "I lost my leg but not my head"). Nicaragua was one of the most incredible, adventurous and welcoming places I've ever been and I hope it's recovering from the difficult times that plagued most of 2018. The people there deserve so much more. Colourful place inside and out.
